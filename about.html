— about —

My name is Spencer Walters. I am an undergradute Computer Engineering student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

I am a teaching assistant for ECE 402 Electronic Music Synthesis—a course taught by Lippold Haken, inventor of the Haken Continuum.

I am currently developing a form of controllable modal synthesis inspired by the flexibility found in older subtractive synthesizers. I also work in a group with two others implementing musical audio algorithms on an Analog Devices SHARC ADSP-21489 digital signal processor.
